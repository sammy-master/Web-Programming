About Our Project

This games website was developed as part of our college project exhibition by a team of four students from the Computer Science department. Our goal was to create a collection of classic games that demonstrate our web development and programming skills while providing an enjoyable experience for users.

Project Objectives

  • Demonstrate proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Implement various game algorithms and mechanics
  • Create an engaging user experience
  • Showcase our ability to work as a team on a complex project
  • Learn and apply modern web development techniques

We hope you enjoy playing our games as much as we enjoyed creating them!
